## Interest

- $p = (1+rt)$
- $p = (1+r)^t$ $\implies$ $p = (1+\frac{r}{n})^{nt}$

## Returns

### ROI formula

$$
ROI = \frac{Net\ return\ on\ Investment}{Cost\ of\ Investment}\times 100
$$

There is an important variant of calculating returns using natural log `ln` to remove positve bias.
